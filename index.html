About us

Meet the Team

Glory Racers is a karting team founded in September 2024 in Žilina out of a pure passion for cars, speed, and racing. Our goal is to become the most successful amateur team and inspire motorsports enthusiasts throughout Slovakia. In 2025, as amateurs, we are participating in the 4-hour endurance series at the Slovakia Ring, where we are currently doing very well. We are currently in third place in the amateur category.

We specialize in both indoor and outdoor karting using SODIKART GT4R karts equipped with 9-horsepower HONDA GX 270 engines. We have a track record of successful races in the SWS Sodi series, where we have already secured a victory and a third-place finish.

The team consists of three drivers: Ondro Mišák, Michal Chochuľ, and Branislav Galuška. Experts from Karting Zone Žilina take care of the technical condition of our karts.
